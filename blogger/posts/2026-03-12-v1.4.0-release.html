v1.4.0 Release Notes

Claude Bridge v1.4.0 adds voice reply, a full voice settings panel, ElevenLabs integration, and automatic sensitive data masking.

Voice Reply — Bot Talks Back

Send a voice message to the bot, and it now replies with both text and voice. Your voice is transcribed locally via Whisper, sent to Claude, and the response is converted to speech.

Two TTS engines are supported:

  • edge-tts — free, fast (~1.4s), 8 Chinese voices including Liaoning and Shaanxi dialects
  • ElevenLabs — cloud API, natural-sounding voices with the eleven_v3 model. Sarah v3 delivers excellent Chinese speech quality

New: /voice — Voice Settings Panel

Full control over voice reply from Telegram:

  • Toggle voice reply on/off
  • Switch engine between edge-tts and ElevenLabs with one tap
  • Select voice from 8 edge-tts or 5 ElevenLabs voices
  • Live preview — tap Preview next to any voice to hear a sample before selecting

All settings persist to SQLite and take effect immediately — no restart needed.

ElevenLabs won on quality and latency for cloud use. Edge-tts remains the best free option. Both are available as switchable engines.

Sensitive Data Masking

Passwords, tokens, and credentials mentioned in your messages are now automatically masked in bot responses. Two-layer detection: exact match from user input + keyword-proximity scanning in responses.

Other Improvements

  • Streaming subprocess buffer increased from 64KB to 4MB — fixes truncation of large tool results
